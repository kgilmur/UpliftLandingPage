Full Integration

View and write new posts anoymously, or log in with Twitter or Facebook to keep track of what you write, and manage it in the profile tab. Uplift is fully integrated with Twitter and Facebook, so it's easy to log in and out.

Native Share

Bring inspiration to someones day! Share your own posts, or others posts with our native sharing feature, enabling access to all your installed applications, such as Facebook, Twitter, Flickr, Google, and Mail!

Real Time

Post as many stories as you want, with the support of Firebase, a real-time database allowing for cross device compatibility.
